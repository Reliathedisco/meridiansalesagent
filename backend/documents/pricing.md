# Meridian Analytics — Pricing & Plans

Meridian offers three plans designed to scale with your team. All plans include a 14-day free trial — no credit card required.

## Starter Plan — $49/month

Best for small teams and early-stage startups getting started with product analytics.

- Up to **1 million events/month**
- **3 team seats** included
- **30-day data retention**
- Core analytics: event tracking, funnels, basic dashboards
- Email support (48-hour response time)
- Community Slack access

Additional seats: $10/seat/month. Event overage: $5 per additional 100K events.

## Growth Plan — $199/month

Best for scaling teams that need deeper insights and API access.

- Up to **20 million events/month**
- **10 team seats** included
- **12-month data retention**
- Everything in Starter, plus:
  - Full REST API access
  - Cohort segmentation
  - AI anomaly detection
  - Warehouse integrations (BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift)
  - Slack and Teams alerting
  - Priority email support (12-hour response time)

Additional seats: $15/seat/month. Event overage: $3 per additional 100K events.

## Enterprise Plan — Custom Pricing

For organizations with advanced security, compliance, or scale requirements.

- **Unlimited events**
- **Unlimited seats**
- **Custom data retention** (up to 5 years)
- Everything in Growth, plus:
  - SSO (SAML 2.0 / OIDC)
  - SCIM user provisioning
  - Dedicated account manager
  - Custom SLA (up to 99.99% uptime)
  - On-premise deployment option
  - Audit logs and RBAC
  - Phone and Slack Connect support
  - Quarterly business reviews

Contact sales@meridian.io for a custom quote.

## Billing & Payment

- All plans are billed monthly. Annual billing is available at a 20% discount.
- We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and wire transfer (Enterprise only).
- Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month.
- Downgrade or cancel anytime — no long-term contracts on Starter or Growth.

## Non-Profit and Education Discount

Registered non-profits and educational institutions receive 50% off any plan. Contact support@meridian.io with proof of status to apply.
